SUTCLIFFE: What a beautiful speech. The rhythm and, and vocabulary, quite

DOWELL: (chief henchman) When, sir?  
SUTCLIFFE: Now! In daylight.

DOCTOR: Afraid it has a knack to it. (Bill watches the hole seal up again.)

BILL: I, I
